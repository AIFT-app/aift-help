# Inviting Team Members

To give someone access to a workspace, you send them an invitation. They receive a link, click it, and are added to the workspace.

---

## Who can send invitations

- **Accountant Admins** can invite both accountants and clients to any workspace in their organization.
- **Client Owners** can invite additional members to their own workspace.

---

## Sending an invitation

When you create an invitation, you provide two things:

- **Email address** — the email the invitee will use to sign in
- **Role** — whether the person joins as an Accountant or a Client

Once you submit the form, an invitation link is generated.

!!! note "Email delivery"
    Automated invitation emails are not yet sent by the system. After creating the invitation, copy the link from the invitation list and share it with the person directly — via email, Slack, or however you normally communicate with them.

---

## Invitation lifecycle

- Invitations are valid for **7 days**.
- If an invitation expires before the person accepts it, you can revoke it and create a new one.
- You can revoke a pending invitation at any time from the invitation list.

---

## The accept flow

When the invitee opens the link:

1. They are taken to the AI Finance Team sign-in page.
2. If they already have an account, they log in. If not, they sign up — the email address is pre-filled.
3. After signing in or signing up, the invitation is accepted automatically and they are taken to the workspace.

!!! warning "Email must match"
    The invitation is tied to the email address you entered. If the invitee tries to accept it from a different account, the invitation will not be accepted. Make sure you invite the correct email address.

---

## After acceptance

Once accepted, the new member appears in the workspace member list with their assigned role. Accountants see the workspace on their cross-workspace dashboard immediately. Clients can access their workspace and begin uploading invoices.
