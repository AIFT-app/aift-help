# Changing Your Language

AI Finance Team is available in **English**, **Hungarian (Magyar)**, and **German (Deutsch)**. Your choice is personal — it changes the interface for you, and the emails we send you, without affecting anyone else in the workspace.

## Switching language

1. Open **Account → Preferences**.
2. Under **Language**, pick English, Magyar, or Deutsch.
3. Click **Save preferences**.

The interface switches straight away, and you'll see a **"Language preference saved."** confirmation. As the page describes: *"Choose the language for the AI Finance Team interface. Emails we send you will use the same language."*

## What it affects

- **The interface** — menus, labels, buttons, and help text throughout the app.
- **Emails from us** — notifications, invitations, and password resets arrive in your chosen language.
- **Number and date formatting** follows the language too (for example, Hungarian groups thousands with a space).

It does **not** change your data. Invoice text, partner names, categories you've created, and amounts are shown exactly as they were entered — only the app's own wording changes.

## Before you sign in

You don't have to be logged in to read the app in your language. The **login, sign-up, and password-reset pages** have a language picker in the corner, and the app does its best to match your browser's language automatically.

If you were **invited** to a workspace, the invitation email arrives in the language the person who invited you chose for you — so your very first contact is already in the right language. You can change it any time afterwards from **Account → Preferences**.

## Don't see your language?

Only English, Hungarian, and German are available today. If you pick a language that isn't ready yet, the app will tell you (*"That language is not available yet."*) and keep your current one. More languages may follow as AI Finance Team expands.
